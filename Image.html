State transition path digraph

Using these 16 states, we established a logic for state transitions. Individuals can transition from unborn to young, young to old, young to dead at a lower rate, and old to dead at a higher rate. Based on this logic, we created a directed graph representing transitions from the initial state (both individuals unborn) to the final state (both individuals dead). From this graph, we identified 126 paths from start to end, with states represented numerically: 0 for unborn, 1 for young, 2 for old, and 3 for deceased.
